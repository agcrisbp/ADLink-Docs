This is the minimal directory structure to get an up and running ADLink.

```bash filename="ADLink"
┣ 📂 components/
┃ ┗ 📁 icons
┃ ┗ 📄 Layout.js
┃ ┗ 📄 Online.tsx
┃ ┗ 📄 ReusableStyles.js
┃ ┗ 📄 Seo.js
┃ ┗ 📄 WebLinks.js
┣ 📂 data/
┃ ┗ 📄 BioData.js
┃ ┗ 📄 LinksData.js
┣ 📁 public
┣ 📄 nuxt-seo.config.js
```

<Callout type="info">
- The `components/WebLinks.js` is your [Frontend Configuration](../frontend).
- The `data/` directory is where you [write your data](../update-info#update-biodata-linksdata).
- The `public` folder is where all the photos are stored.
- The `nuxt-seo.config.js` is your [SEO Configuration](../seo).
</Callout>
