`ADLink` is a perfect custom landing page for your links on the world wide web.

Usually, you don't need to worry a lot about coding if you're just updating information in `next-seo.config.js`, `BioData.js` and `LinkData.js`. To deep dive know more  [Next.js](https://nextjs.org/docs) and [React](https://reactjs.org/getting-started.html) official documentation.
<Callout type="info">
Template auto support dark-mode depending on system config.
